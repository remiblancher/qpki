# Governance

This project is currently maintained and governed by Remi Blancher.

## Decision-making

The project maintainer has final authority over:
- the project roadmap
- acceptance and rejection of contributions
- release management
- licensing decisions
- governance changes

## Contributions

Contributions are welcome and reviewed by the maintainer.
Submitting a contribution does not grant any decision-making
authority over the project.

All contributions are subject to the Contributor License Agreement (CLA).

## Transition to a company

Upon creation of a company to operate this project, governance
will be transferred to the company, which will assume the role
of project owner and maintainer.
